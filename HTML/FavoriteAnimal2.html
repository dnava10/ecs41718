Hedgehogs are mammals that live about seven years. They are carinvores that live in grasslands, woodlands, and in meadows. Hedgehogs are known for their prickly spines which they use for defense when they sleep and when they face enemies. There are fifteen different species of hedgehog. They are found around Europe, Asia, and Africa.
